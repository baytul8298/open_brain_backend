About Me

I am a passionate Mathematics teacher with over 7 years of experience in secondary and higher secondary education. I specialize in making abstract mathematical concepts tangible and accessible for students of all ability levels — from those struggling with basic algebra to those preparing for national board exams and university admissions.

My approach is built on three pillars: conceptual clarity first, then systematic practice, and finally exam strategy. I believe every student can excel in Mathematics with the right guidance, structured repetition, and genuine encouragement.

I have successfully guided over 300 students through SSC and HSC examinations, with 87% of my regular students achieving A or A+ grades in Mathematics. I am committed to delivering the same quality of teaching online as I do in the classroom.
